About Us

Founded in 2013, our company has built a solid reputation in the construction and repair industry through numerous commercial and residential projects across Ukraine, Russia, and Kazakhstan. Due to the military actions in Ukraine, we made the strategic decision to relocate our operations entirely to the United States, where our business continues to flourish.

As a family-owned enterprise managed by two dedicated owners, we are committed to delivering top-quality repair, construction, and home improvement services tailored to our clients' needs. We are fully insured and hold all the necessary licenses to carry out our work safely and professionally.

Our focus is on enhancing living conditions and ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of excellence.
